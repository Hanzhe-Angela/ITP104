Facts about my name

My Chinese name is 陈含哲, pronounced as Chen Hanzhe. Chen is my family name. My grandfather bestowed upon me the name Han, which means 'to have.' Interestingly, this character is shared with my cousin. This practice is common in China, where we have a tradition of children of the same generation and gender sharing the same middle Chinese character in their names. To ensure that both my cousin's and my names each contain two 口 characters, my father named me Zhe, signifying 'philosophy.' Hence, my name implies 'to hold or possess philosophy.' It's a unique name, and I've yet to meet someone with the same one.
