Facts about my name

My Chinese name is 陈含哲 Chen Hanzhe. Chen is my family name, and my grandfather granted me the name of Han, meaning to have, the same Chinese character as my cousin, which is casual in China because here we have the tradition of kids in same generations of same gender have the same middle Chinese character. Then to fulfill the trick that my cousin and I both have two 口 characters in the name, my father granted me the name of zhe, meaning philosophy. So my name means to imply philosophy. This is really a cool name and I never see anyone having the same name as I do.
